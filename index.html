Production?

The production of the first game was based on the original idea that Ed Boon and John Tobias had to make a game in which Jean-Claude Van Damme participated, but the idea was left out, and instead Mortal Kombat was created, a game of wrestling with fantasy and science themes, released in October 1992.

Developer(s):

Mortal Kombat is a series of games created by the Chicago studio of Midway Games. In 2011, after the bankruptcy of Midway, the production of Mortal Kombat was acquired by Warner Bros, then becoming Netherealm. Warner currently owns series rights.
